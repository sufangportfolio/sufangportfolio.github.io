“Literacy is integrally related to learning in all areas of the curriculum, and enables all individuals to develop knowledge and understanding. Reading and writing, when integrated with speaking, listening, viewing and critical thinking, constitutes valued aspects of literacy in modern life.”

Main goal:

In my literacy classroom, I provide a balanced literacy program with rich language experience. A range of literacy skills and knowledge are explicitly taught through demonstration and modelling. I centre my literacy program on the following 8 areas:

  • Building relevant oral language to support literacy
  • Awareness that texts are written for a range of purposes
  • Word meaning and vocabulary knowledge
  • Phonological/phonemic knowledge, orthographic/morphemic knowledge
  • Reading aloud to achieve fluency and phrasing
  • Literal, critical, inferential and creative comprehension outcomes
  • Recognising and using forms, linguistic structures and features of written texts
  • The use of metacognitive and self-management strategies
