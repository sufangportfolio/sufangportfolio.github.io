In my math lessons, I aim to provide my students with:

  • Opportunities for all children to explain their mathematical thinking
  • A variety of learning opportunities
  • A range of representations of the same concept
  • A balance of explicit teaching, jointly constructed understandings and independent learning
  • A growing sense of mastery in numeracy

I use the following strategies:

  • Use of concrete and tangible objects
  • Visual cues and presentations 
  • Combine with Science
  • Guided Tasks
  • Roleplay/ Humanised Mathematics 
  • ICT Activities, Presentations and Responses
  • Real World Questions and Problems
  • Scaffolding
  • Open Ended Problems/Tasks
