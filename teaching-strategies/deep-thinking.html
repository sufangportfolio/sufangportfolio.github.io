Deep thinking

  • Create a classroom culture that encourages growth mindset
  • Clear learning intentions – WALT, WILF, TIB
  • Make learning more relevant (e.g. Math unit on money, show pic of supermarket, create shops in investivation, 3 act math)
  • Visual thinking chart – 5 levels and teach students to identify which stage they are at and what they need to do to progress
  • IDEA, CAFÉ and Writing process chart
  • SOLO Taxonomy
  • Graphic organizers e.g. PMI, Venn diagram, flow chart, Y chart etc.
  • Meaning classroom discussion using strategies such as Teach-OK, Concentric Circles, Fishbowl and Think-Pair-Share
  • Thinkers Keys – during investigation, e.g. students brainstorm how many ways they can use to create a set of wheels. They wonder what if the world is in black and white. They discuss the advantage and disadvantages of certain materials such as different uses of bottle caps. They use the Alphabet Key to brainstorm what they know about subjects.
